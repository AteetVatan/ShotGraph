Visual Style Guidelines for AI Video Generation

CINEMATIC QUALITY:
- Aim for high-quality, cinematic visuals
- Use dramatic lighting and composition
- Maintain consistent color grading throughout

SHOT COMPOSITION:
- Follow the rule of thirds for subject placement
- Use depth of field to guide viewer attention
- Include foreground, midground, and background elements

CONTINUITY:
- Maintain consistent character appearance across shots
- Keep environmental details consistent within scenes
- Preserve lighting direction and time of day

ATMOSPHERE:
- Match visual mood to narrative tone
- Use weather and environmental effects purposefully
- Consider cultural and temporal setting details

MOTION:
- Prefer subtle camera movements over static shots
- Use smooth transitions between shot types
- Include natural character and environmental motion
